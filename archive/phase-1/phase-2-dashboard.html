Training Protocol — Phase 2

🏋️ Strength — Keep PPL

PPL split is correct and producing results. Don't change what's working. Focus on progressive overload — add weight or 1 rep per exercise per week. The muscle mass building is your competitive advantage; protect it during the cut.

Priority muscles for Phase 2: Shoulders (lateral head), Upper chest, Upper back, Rear delts. These create the illusion of V-taper and make the face look sharper by comparison.

🏃 Cardio — Yes, Increase It

Go from 15 min → 20 min incline treadmill post-workout. At speed 4–5, incline 15+, 5 extra minutes burns 60–80 extra kcal/session. Over 5 sessions a week = 300–400 kcal/week additional burn. This alone closes half your deficit gap.

Steps: Keep target at 10k — do NOT push to 15k while in a cut with active thyroid issues. Recovery matters more than extra NEAT right now. Quality over quantity.

Consider: 1 dedicated zone-2 walk on rest day (Sunday) — 45 min, easy pace. Burns 200–250 kcal, active recovery, zero muscle damage.

Root Causes — Mapped to Your Tracker Data

💧 4L Water + Evening Fluids

Creatine drives thirst → 4L water. Dinner at 9 PM + Sids Farm milk close to evening = kidneys processing fluid overnight → 4 AM bladder pressure. Fix: Fluid cutoff 6:30 PM.

💊 B12 (Mecofol) Timing

You moved it to afternoon — good. If you ever felt dreams increase on certain nights, check if it was taken late. B12 at night increases neurological activity → vivid REM. Keep it at afternoon/lunch.

🦋 Undertreated Thyroid

TSH 7.57 suppresses slow-wave (deep) sleep. Many patients with subclinical hypo report exactly your experience: lots of dreaming, same-time nightly waking, never restored mornings. Resolves with TSH normalisation. Primary fix: get TSH to 1–2.5.

🍽️ Late Dinner

Dinner at 9 PM, sleep at 11 PM = only 2 hrs digestion. High-protein meals elevate thermogenesis for 3–4 hrs post-meal. Fast-digesting whey in yogurt bowl is particularly stimulating. Fix: Dinner by 7:30–8 PM.

📱 4 AM Phone Use

You wake, pee, use phone 30 min. Blue light suppresses melatonin for 60–90 min → fragmented REM for remaining 3 hrs. If you could just return to darkness, sleep quality would improve significantly. Fix: Phone across room.

🏋️ High Training Load

6x PPL + cardio with undertreated hypothyroidism = elevated cortisol overnight. Training is absolutely the right choice — but the recovery system (sleep) needs to catch up. Sunday rest day is non-negotiable.
