```

  └── Phases[]
        ├── Phase 1: Jan 8 – Mar 31, 2026 [COMPLETE — READ ONLY]

        ├── Phase 2B: Jul 1 – Oct 30, 2026 [UPCOMING]

  ├── Metadata (name, dates, boss battle, targets, status)
```
